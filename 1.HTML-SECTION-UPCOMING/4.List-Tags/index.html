Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Add eggs; beat until combined
  3. Drop rounded tablespoons of dough on to baking sheets
